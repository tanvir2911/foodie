About Me

I'm greatly fascinated by food culture. The magnificent ingredients in the delicacies engross me more than I would be happy writing on something!! The smell of those chilli potatoes of my mom's hand enlighten my heart and soul and make me run up to the kitchen to look the way she prepares it! But because being a foodie is about living with your 5-senses on full blast at every single meal. What I feel is :- Being a foodie isn’t just about food. It’s about how the food, makes you feel. For us, it’s not just about satiating our hunger. Each meal is a celebration. A delight. A party in our mouth. A feast for our senses.
